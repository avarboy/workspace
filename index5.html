Low Calorie Pizza

This low calorie pizza recipe has just 50 calories per slice and less than 400 calories

Ingredients

  • 2 cup all purpose flour
  • 100 ml tomato ketchup
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 onion
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Take a dough kneading plate and add all-purpose flour to it.
  2. Next, add salt and baking powder in it and sieve the flour once.
  3. Then, make a well in the centre and add 2 teaspoon of olive oil to it.
  4. On the other hand, take a little warm water and mix the yeast in it along with 1 teaspoon of sugar
  5. Mix well and keep aside for 10-15 minutes.
  6. The yeast will rise in the meantime.
  7. Once the yeast has risen, add it to the flour knead the dough nicely using some water.
